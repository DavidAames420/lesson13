At Eternal Life Charities we dedicate our lives to educating the facts and making the public aware of the growing statistics of the respitory disease that is asthma. Many people suffer from asthma and many people died early deaths due to the lack of knowledge in how to treat, and or prevent and asthma attack. Knowing the facts about asthma can not only save your life but also save you money due to the increasing cost of asthmatic medicines.

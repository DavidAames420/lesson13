Eternal Charities

Eternal Life Charities is a non profit organization designed to help spread awareness of the respiratory disease known as asthma. Asthma is a disease that inflames the lungs and causes shortness of breath. Although asthma is incurable you can learn to keep it under control. Most people with asthma can lead normal lives. However you having an asthma attack can be terrifying for both children and adults. If not taken care of properly, it can be serious and even fatal. Additionally, emergency room visits for asthma sufferers, both young and old, are very common. Even worse, thousands die every year as a direct or indirect result of an asthma attack. Most of these deaths could be avoided with proper treatment. In the United Kingdom, for example, approximately 1400 people every year die from an asthma attack.â€¨
That's about four people a day. In short, people are dying needlessly. If you learn to deal with your asthma, you can not only live a normal life, but you may very well save your own life. If you're an adult and have recently been diagnosed with asthma, make sure you talk to your doctor and get proper information. The Internet is also a fine resource to find information on treatment. However, you should take care that that the sites you get information from are endorsed by the proper professionals and not simply "quack" treatments. Remember that information is power, and if you have the proper information, you're well armed to deal with an asthma attack, should it occur. There are many ways to that an asthma attack can be avoided and this charity is here to give you free information for the public.
